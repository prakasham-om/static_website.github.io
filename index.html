WHY TAJ

Taj Hotel Mumbai boasts of 550 grand rooms and suites complete with an exhaustive list of amenities. Available on both smoking and non-smoking floors, the spacious interiors include 40-inch flat-screen TVs, Posturepedic mattresses, pillow and bath menus and plush bathrobes and slippers for the added comfort of guests.

They include a spa, outdoor pool, 24-hour fitness centre concierge service and multilingual staff, safe deposit lockers and 24-hour laundry, to name a select few.

Along with the Taj Mumbai's amenities and facilities, the hotel is known for its restaurants that are often frequented by politicians and celebrities.
